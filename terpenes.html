YOFUMO TERPENE APPLICATION FRAMEWORK ©

Our patented decontamination technology creates a clean environment allowing for the use of both classical and modern finishing and curing techniques. Our technology also opens the door for continued development of revolutionary terpene applications.

Yofumo offers the industry’s first-ever terpene management framework and precision application techniques for raw biomass.

All of our terpenes are %100 organic non-GMO derived and are available in shelf, house, and custom blends. Yofumo’s terpenes are not only precisely tailored to work with your Yofumo unit and supporting hardware; they are also precisely calibrated and designed to be seamlessly incorporated into both distillate and hydrocarbon based concentrate applications eliminating the need for blending as well as viscosity problems. Pioneered by Yofumo, these new terpene management techniques ensure a perfect application every time, regardless of weight or volume.

All of our Terpenes originate from 100% NON-GMO, and organically sourced from non-cannabis feed-stock.
